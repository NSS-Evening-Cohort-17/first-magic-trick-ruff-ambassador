Locust

2305 12th Ave S, Nashville, TN 37204

My Favorite Dishes

steamed pork dumplings with chili oil

royal red shrimp pocket

beef tartare, warm rice, nori sheet

vegetables with dipping salt

One of the most inventive and interesting spots I've tried in a long time. I love that the menu is small and curated. Everything is meant to be eaten in waves, so we just ordered the whole menu. The dumplings were amazing, but my favorite was the beef tartare. Really enjoyed the highball cocktails, too!
